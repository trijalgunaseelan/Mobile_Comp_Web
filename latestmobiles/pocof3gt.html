POCO F3 GT

The Poco F3 GT boasts of powerful hardware. It is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 1200 SoC and is offered in 6GB and 8GB RAM options, both with 128GB of storage, and there's also an 8GB RAM, 256GB storage option. I had the mid-spec variant with 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage for this review.
